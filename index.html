About

Bow & Crescent is a tax advisory firm serving an international clientele at the intersection of private equity, venture capital, and digital assets.

Our founder has spent over two decades working at the investor and venture capital level — first in Big Four public accounting, then embedded within leading digital asset VC funds. That firsthand experience on the investment side means we understand tax not as an abstract compliance exercise, but as a core component of fund strategy and portfolio value creation.

We specialize in the tax challenges unique to fund managers, LPs, and portfolio companies operating across US and international jurisdictions — as well as the broader digital asset tax landscape, from token economics and DeFi protocols to on-chain reporting and evolving regulatory frameworks.

Services

US Tax Compliance & Planning

Federal and state tax compliance for individuals and entities with US tax obligations, including FATCA reporting, treaty-based planning, and partnership tax matters.

International Tax Structuring

Cross-border holding structures, transfer pricing considerations, and treaty optimization for multinational investment platforms and their principals.

Digital Asset Tax Advisory

Tax treatment of tokens, DeFi protocols, staking, and on-chain transactions across multiple jurisdictions. Guidance on emerging regulatory requirements and reporting obligations.

Private Equity Fund Tax Services

Structuring, carried interest planning, management fee arrangements, and investor tax reporting for private equity and venture capital funds investing in digital assets.
